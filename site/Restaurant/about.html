About Us

Arizona's #1 mobile detail service.

Experience

Serving the Phoenix metro area for over 10 years, providing customers with basic washes all the way to complete details. Our current clients consist of both residential clients and companies with fleets. Some of the clients we have served in the past are, GM Financial, Frontier Shop Supplies, Casino AZ, Univision, Kona Ice, Bulletproof Diesel, Sun Valley Church, Solar City, Wildflower Bread Company, and many local used car dealerships.

Quality First

We only use the best products we can find to maintain your vehicle. The products found at your local store or auto store are OK, but not professional grade here at stay shining we recommend staying away from those products. You will not see any of those things on our trucks. Our major product line we use is distributed by a local car wash supplier who supplies detail shops and car washes around the valley. We also use brands like Maguire's, Pro Beauty, and Groits Garage. Everything that is used is 100% safe to apply to your vehicle.

Professional

The detail or wash you will be receiving is a professional grade service, the same type of service you would expect from any professional detail shop or car wash. We show up to the appointments we schedule, you will not have to worry about tracking us down to get a job done once we have you scheduled. Your car is a huge piece of your life and you should feel safe with it in someone else's hands. With our professional approach you can rest assured we will take good care of your huge investment. When the job is complete we clean up and pick up the surrounding area as well as if we were never there.

Satisfaction

We want to leave all of our customers not just happy but in love with their vehicle again. We will not leave a job unless the customer is more than pleased with the work. Once the job is complete a Stay Shining detail tech will walk the vehicle with the customer to ensure the customers satisfaction, and nothing was missed, during this process future services may recommended. We hope to build a relationship with you and your car and not just see you the one time, the only way for that to happen is to deliver amazing and high quality work satisfaction guaranteed.

Equipment

When you call Stay Shining I am sure one of the main reasons your calling is to experience the convenience of a truly mobile wash or detail. Convenience is what we strive for that's why we. We get called out for a job we show up ready to work, not just with the right attitude to freshen up your vehicle, but also the right equipment. Our wash vehicles are fully equipped with at least 125 gallons of water, 2000 watts of power, vacuums, carpet extractors, and high speed buffers. We require nothing but access to your vehicle to get the job done. We will not need to ever ask a customer for power or water. Being mobile means being able to provide a service anywhere and that's what we can do.

Local Business

Stay Shining is a local business, we have been servicing primarily the Phoenix east valley for over 10 years. There are many benefits of spending your money with a local small business. 1st off is the money spent stays in the community. What's better than keeping your money in the local economy ? Also you are investing in the growth of your community. By supporting local business you promote growth of the business creating more jobs for people in the community. Most of all small local businesses thrive on repeat customers and those customers telling everyone about them, you can usually expect a high quality product when dealing with a local business that's no different with Stay Shining we depend on repeat business and all your friends and family hearing about us, so we deliver the best product we possibly can on every job.
