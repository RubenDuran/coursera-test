Is car detailing and washing the same?

Car detailing and car washing are two entirely different things. A good way to look at it is, a wash is kind of like maintenance. A wash should be done regularly to protect the paint of the vehicle.Road tar, bug guts, fine dust and tree sap are just a few of the things your vehicle encounter daily, and need to be removed. A wash includes an interior vacuum, a wipe down of the interior and a window cleaning, it's a very basic cleaning of day to day mess.

A detail is a very in depth cleaning of the entire vehicle from bumper to bumper. The goal of a detail should be to clean the car to a like new status. A detail usually will include a wash of the exterior. Tires and wheels cleaned and shined, Wheel walls cleaned and shined, Windows cleaned inside and out, wax or polish applied, engine cleaned, interior vacuumed thoroughly underneath seats, behind seats and between seats. Cup holders, pockets, cubby areas, door panels all cleaned, door jams wiped down, carpets shampooed, seats shampooed (cleaned and conditioned if leather), floor mats shampooed.

Very easy to see there are huge differences between a wash and a detail. Though they may be different they are both needed to maintain your vehicles beauty and value.
